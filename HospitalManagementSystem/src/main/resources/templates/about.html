About Us

Health Care Diagnostic Center Limited (Formerly known as Health Care Diagnostic Center Private Limited) is India’s largest comprehensive diagnostic network with over 90 state-of-the-art centers spread across 15 cities.Health Care Diagnostic Center has a qualified team of over 2200 professionals consisting of some of the country’s top radiologists, pathologists and healthcare professionals. This has enabled oriented diagnostic services that help us in establishing trust and reliability with our patients.


Our Story

With a vision of providing comprehensive, innovative and state-of-the-art diagnostic services under one roof, in a reliable, affordable and customer-centric manner, Dr.S.Vikram Singh founded Health Care Diagnostic Center in 1982. In the past 4 decades,Health Care Diagnostic Center has constantly worked towards providing excellent quality throughout all its centers and has been the pioneer in using the latest technological trends to deliver best-in-class health care to its patients. Today, Health Care Diagnostic Center has expanded to over 63 Centers in Delhi, in addition to other branches in Sangareddy, Warangal, Hanmakonda, Karimnagar, Nizamabad, Kurnool, Nellore, Visakhapatnam, Kolkata and Gurgaon.
